Daily Tips

MyTippingApp is a next generation tipping service that scans the Internet for similarities in trusted tipsters suggestions and ranks them. The software then applies our unique algorithm to drill down even further eliminating the high-risk races. The software looks at the horse’s recent form by combining track data relating to Distance, Weight, Jockey, Recent wins, Barrier position and speed maps.

Live Prices

Well we have thought about that as well. MyTippingApp access live pricing data from the QLD TAB and updates every 5 seconds ensuring you have the best information.

Staking Calculator

It is well known in racing that level staking is not the most effective way to create long term success. So therefore we have included a Staking Calculator. The calculator will ask you whether you are aiming at making a total cash return for the race or a % return on investment. The Calculator will them tell you how much to put on each runner. If you can’t achieve your goal or a cash return or % return the app will tell you that as well.

What about prices I hear you say. Do I need to manually put them in? Nope we have thought of that as well.
